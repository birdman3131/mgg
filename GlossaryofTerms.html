Aberrant Anathema

The most dangerous kind. MG need to be 100 levels above to fight on even ground.


Abilities

A non modifiable active skill that either costs mana or has a cooldown.


Aligned

Refers to an item that receives benefits from stats.


Anathema

The bad aliens.


Attuned

Refers to an item that can be enchanted.


Augments

Passive abilities or body modifications that cost mana regen to operate.


Arcane gate

Used to buy stuff with Points.


Curse Bearer

An Anathema that fused with a Curse Seed.


Curse Seed

What keeps Usurpations active. Destroy the seed and the usurpation ends.


Elite Anathema

Ones at a higher level then normal.

Familiar

A magical construct that is an AI that is supposed to help humanity.  Every MG has a familiar, and there are also unattached ones.


Geas

A magical contract making impossible to divulge the details contained, even if tortured or by other means.


Grimoires

Catalogs of spells that can be purchased for points.


Magitech

A combination of magic and advanced technology.


Manaborn

Manaborn are someone who was exposed to mana when very young or in the fetal stage.


Miasma

Negative mana. Magic with a negative alignment.


Mundane

Refers to something that can be made by humans without Zenith tech.


MG

Officially stands for Magical Guardian, but some MG go by Magical Girl instead.


Origin

The color system.


Rare Anathema

Similar to boss monsters.


Reality Usurpation

When anathema attack.

 

Rebellion

A usurpation where the Anathema open the rift from earths reality. Known to be significantly more dangerous.

 

Shadow

A magical construct that every magic user eventually develops, that is supposed to help that magic user deal with trauma.  Also the name of Mai's shadow.


Signature/signature magic

A entirely unique to each individual MG magic ability that all MG have. (No two are the same)


Silverite

Silverite Is a metal alloy that poisons Anathema by disrupting the miasma of their bodies.


Soul Gem

A brain implant given to MG that allows them to use magic and houses their familiar.


Unaligned

Refers to something that receives no benefits from stats.


Unattuned

Refers to something that can’t be enchanted.


Vaults

Catalogs of physical items that can be purchased for Points.


Wardens

Elite Magical Soldiers.


Zenith

The good aliens.
