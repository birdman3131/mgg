Some MGs are loners.  Some MGs are organized into teams.  The only team names we know of are Fantasy Fatale and Magical Cakes.

Some MGs belong to guilds.  The only guild we currently know the name of is Maid Cafe.

The only two teams we have seen have four members.  We do not know if all teams have four members, however when the MGs were attacking the Anathema heading back to the shielding station in ch.25, they were in “groups of four to eight.”

The benefit of being a member of a team is obvious;  we do not know what guilds do for their members.
