The first MGs were created just over 21 years ago.  MGs are grouped into seven year long “generations”.   These are as arbitrary as grouping people by decades.

Mika posted the following in #word-of-god in discord:

Please note the following numbers are what I use as REFERENCE. They may be adjusted in the future up to the point where they are officially mentioned in the story. Generally, about 1,632 new magical girls are added per year. About 1,386 (198 x 7) of them end up attending one of the schools, the rest are mentored, run solo, or attend the next year depending when they were added. There have been 34,624 contracts. Only 18,620 are still active duty. 5,103 are “retired.” This leaves 2,660 per region roughly. Each Sanctuary city accounts for almost 300 Guardians, not counting recruits, but worldwide portal network lets them deploy quickly.

As for current generational Numbers (each magical girl generation is denotated by a 7 year period):
1st Gen: 3,253 + 907 Retired (Generation: Hero)
2nd Gen: 6,602 + 2,805 Retried (Generation: Warrior)
3rd Gen: 8,765 + 1,391 Retired (Generation: Idol)
4th Gen: 1,386 (Current Gen, no name)

Note that the 18,620 figure is the total for the first three generations.  None of the current generation is in that figure.

(Note also that this means the Breakdance, although an MG for less than a year, is in the 3rd, not the 4th gen.)
