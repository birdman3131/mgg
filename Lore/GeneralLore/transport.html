In addition to the usual means of transport, there is some teleportation possible.  The Guardian Spires all have teleportation ritual rooms, as do some other locations (Mikasane #mgg-general 11/11/2024 1:12 PM), and some MGs have the ability to teleport themselves and others (Mikasane #mgg-general 9/10/2023 11:55 AM).

Most MGs do not teleport;  for example, Fantasy Fatale uses a helicopter to get around.

Arcadia has an extensive subway and monorail network.
