Classifications:


Unattuned

These are mundane weapons made without Zenith tech. They can’t be enchanted.


Unaligned

Receives no benefit from stats.


Attuned

Able to be enchanted, and or is magitech.


Aligned

Damage grows with stats.


 

Alignment:


Strength:

Example. “a strength-aligned sword would make it grow sharper, sturdier, and heavier to offset your strength bonus. It would essentially always feel the same weight to you, but in reality, it would be heavier and therefore more potent as you swung it at opponents” Selene ch.6


 

Dexterity:

Example. “for a bow and arrow. Faster and stronger projectiles with more kinetic power. The weapon itself would also gain some benefits to durability.” Selene ch.6
