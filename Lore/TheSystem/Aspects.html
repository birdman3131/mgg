Mika gave the following information about aspects on discord, 7/23/2024, 10:53 PM:

Aspect Bonuses: What would a Non-Dark MG's look like? Well, let me copy and paste directly from my notes: 

Very Generalized Aspect Bonus Distribution for Non-Dark MGS

Level 1: Specific Spell Type Bonus (Conjuration, Single Target, Aoe, Healing, etc.)
Level 10: Bonus Experience for Specific Actions (Scouting, Smithing, Healing, Singing, etc.)
Level 25: Specific Spell Elemental Bonus Level 50: Bonus Mana Efficiency for Specific Actions
Level 100: Specific Weapon Specialization (Stronger, Piercing, Easier to Wield, etc.)
Level 250: Bonus Spell Power for Specific Actions
Level 500: (HIDDEN)

Generally percentage increases are ~10% NOTE: This is not a golden rule, it is a general one that I use for side-characters. MGs can deviate quite a bit from this rule, even non-Darks. Dark's almost always do, and I'm thinking they usually if not always get a special passive Skill (Augment) like Mai did.

As you can see by the generalized Aspect Bonuses, they are very much more spread out to give the Guardian nice little boosts to a lot of different things. A Dark's Aspect Bonus generally revolves around one singular theme. For Mai it is vision and sin. For another it could be cutting things, breaking things apart, being unseen, etc.
