More on Sloth:

If Mai has looked at a map of a location, it can accurately depict it as well as update it in real time based on her location and the direction she is facing. Waypoint can placed be on it, as well as arrows can appear in her vision guiding her to the target.

Anywhere Selene scouts while summoned is filled out on the map in real time, and she can mark the location of Anathema or other targets of interest.

The targeting reticle is shaped as a black circle with a dot in the center that changes to red when targeting an Anathema. The circle will change to accurately predict the area her shots could land in real time. (Smaller when aiming, larger when moving etc.) Also adapts to the type of ammo being used such as shotgun.

Is the only known augment to have absolutely zero Mana cost to use. (As of ch35)

Shows the number of rounds in currently held gun.

Selene gave a minimum cost for augments that have similar but significantly more limited functions. 3000 points for a magic based one, and 5000 for a cybernetic. (Ch.6)
