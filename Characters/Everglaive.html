Signature Magic:

Glade of Glaives

Cost: about 1700 mana.

Protects an area by surrounding it with metallic pine trees that are made out of glaives. The needle glaives can be fired at range while, the limb and root glaives can slash and stab.

There is some confusion here, because Glade is certainly her signature magic, but also seems to be a Fatale-class spell.  One hopes this will be clarified at some point.
