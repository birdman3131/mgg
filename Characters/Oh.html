First seen ch.12


Works for the Arcadian police department second division. She was posted in the Mega Shelter in the mall during the emergence in vol.1.


She is kind, thoughtful, understanding, and warm when interacting with Mai, and has been shown to be extremely protective of her. even after learning Mai is a MG. (mama bear type).


Perceptive enough to notice Mai giving off multiple red flags for things unrelated to what happened while getting to the shelter. (Revealed in a conversation she has with Sergeant Markus in ch.13)

She has given Mai a card that contains her personal contact info that she “only ever given to people she would drop everything to help” (ch.13) and tells Mai that she can contact her any time for any reason. Ch.12

(She has given her personal card 6 other times).
 

Appearance:

“A young Korean woman, with silky onyx hair that fell to the middle of her neck, and a soft face with warm hazel eyes.” Mai ch.12
