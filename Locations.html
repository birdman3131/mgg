General Overview

The story is set on earth, and has the same geography except for the mega cities built by and gifted to humanity by the zenith.

The mega cities Arcadia, Avalon, Atlantis, Elysium, Shangri-la, and Nirvana, are all built out in the ocean. The only one that differs is Eden that is a flying island.  Each has a population upwards of 50 million.  Each has an MG academy.
