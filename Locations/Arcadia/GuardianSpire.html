Guardian Spire, also known as Arcadia Spire, is the location from which MGs, Magical Soldiers, the police, and everyone else is coordinated.  Whether it has command authority over MGs is not clear;  it sort of does and sort of doesn't.

This is where Error Machina works from.

“The Arcadia Spire was directly in the center of the city also made orienting yourself simple, the skyscraper of smooth angles, glass, and helipads standing out with a distinctly futuristic architecture.”  ch 34.

The major internal feature we have seen is a large room that looks like a space mission control center, with a lot of people in front of computer monitors and a giant screen in front of the room. ch 25.

In earlier editions of the story, known as Guardian Command spire, changed to simply Guardian Spire.

Mika stated that Guardian Spire and Arcadia Spire are the same thing on discord mgg-general, 9/13/2024 10:43 PM.
