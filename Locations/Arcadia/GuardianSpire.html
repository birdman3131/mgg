Guardian Spire is the location from which MGs, Magical Soldiers, the police, and everyone else is coordinated.  Whether it has command authority over MGs is not clear;  it sort of does and sort of doesn't.

This is where Error Machina works from.

The major feature we have seen is a large room that looks like a space mission control center, with a lot of people in front of computer monitors and a giant screen in front of the room.

In earlier editions of the story, known as Guardian Command spire, changed to simply Guardian Spire.
