Guardian Spire, also known as Arcadia Spire, is the location from which MGs, Magical Soldiers, the police, and everyone else is coordinated.  Whether the people there have command authority over MGs is not clear;  it sort of does and sort of doesn't.

It is the tallest building in Arcadia, and also fairly wide.  However, it is much larger on the inside, having many rooms that have been expanded using spacial magic. (ch. 36)

This is where Error Machina works from.

“The Arcadia Spire was directly in the center of the city also made orienting yourself simple, the skyscraper of smooth angles, glass, and helipads standing out with a distinctly futuristic architecture.”  ch 34.

A major internal feature we have seen is a large room that looks like a space mission control center, with a lot of people in front of computer monitors and a giant screen in front of the room. ch 25.

The tavern on the tenth floor is designed to look like an adventurer's guild.  (ch. 36)
